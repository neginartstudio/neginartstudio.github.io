about

Negin Hedayat has started fine art years ago when she was 16 years old. She got her fine art and animation diploma 16 years ago back in Canada and She has been involved in film industry since she was 19. She has been teaching Drawing and Painting to children and adults since 12 years ago. She is working toward her master degree in Fine Arts at California State University, Fullerton.

Classes

Negin’s students will receive one on one instruction while they are in a warm and friendly environment. She will go over the fundamentals of drawing and painting each time they want to start new drawing or painting unless they have requested otherwise. All the students will have strong base knowledge about drawing and/or painting and build on top of that, layer by layer, learning the necessary techniques slowly but constantly. This way they practice what they are taught and it stays with them forever. The students will receive group lessons and demonstrations from time to time in the classes as well.

"Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once he/she grows up."

Picasso
